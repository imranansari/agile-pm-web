• sticky, with arrows:This sticky clueTip has its "close" text in the title bar. It won't close until you close it, or until you hover over another clue-tipped link. It also displays an arrow on one of its sides, pointing to the invoking element. sticky clueTip with arrows
    View the HTML
    <a id="sticky" href="ajax6.htm" rel="ajax6.htm">
    View the jQuery
    $('#sticky').cluetip({sticky: true, closePosition: 'title', arrows: true});
  • non-link element, custom attribute, and hover class: Block-level items such as this h4 have clueTips positioned by the mouse.

    Hover over me.

    View the HTML
    <h4 title="Fancy Title!" id="ajax3.htm">Hover over me</h4>
    View the jQuery
    $('h4').cluetip({attribute: 'id', hoverClass: 'highlight'});
  • local, with arrows: This one uses local content from a hidden div element and displays an arrow that points to the invoking element: hover for local
    View the HTML
    <a class="load-local" href="#loadme" rel="#loadme">
    View the jQuery
    $('a.load-local').cluetip({local:true, cursor: 'pointer'});
